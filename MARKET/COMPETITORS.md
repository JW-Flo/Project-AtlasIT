# AtlasIT Competitive Landscape (2026 Expansion)

## Positioning Summary

AtlasIT is positioned as a unified automation platform spanning identity lifecycle, compliance state, security orchestration, third-party assurance, trust center operations, and external exposure management.

## Competitive Adjacency Map

### Compliance + Trust Workflows

- Vanta
- Drata
- SafeBase

### Third-Party Risk / Vendor Assurance

- OneTrust
- Vanta (adjacent)
- Drata (adjacent)

### Exposure Management / Threat Scanning

- Tenable
- Rapid7
- CrowdStrike EASM

## AtlasIT Differentiation Thesis

- One evidence graph across operations, attestations, and exposure findings
- Workflow-native remediation tied to compliance state changes
- Combined internal identity telemetry + external attack-surface telemetry
- Trust Center outputs grounded in live, attributable evidence lineage

## Strategic M&A Attractiveness

This architecture aligns with category extension opportunities relevant to:

- ServiceNow (GRC + workflow + enterprise operations)
- CrowdStrike (external risk + response + identity/security convergence)
- Okta (identity lifecycle + governance + partner ecosystem)
- Vanta / Drata (compliance platform expansion into operational automation and external risk)
