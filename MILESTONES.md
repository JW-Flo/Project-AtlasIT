# AtlasIT Milestones (Next 90 Days)

## Milestone 1 (Days 0–30): Vendor + Trust + Exposure Foundations

- Vendor inventory with ownership and tiering metadata
- Security questionnaire workflows (manual + template import)
- Trust Center MVP with controlled evidence sharing
- Exposure Lite scanner (domain/subdomain/TLS/DNS/headers/open ports)
- Demo flows combining JML + CDT + Trust Center outputs

## Milestone 2 (Days 31–60): Intelligence + Automation

- AI questionnaire response generation from existing evidence graph
- Continuous external monitoring schedules and alerting
- Vendor reassessment cadence automation
- Risk scoring engine v1 (vendor + exposure + compliance linkage)

## Milestone 3 (Days 61–90): Autonomous + Federation

- Autonomous remediation workflows with approval guardrails
- Dependency-aware compliance simulation prior to remediation execution
- MSSP federation controls for multi-tenant operations and delegated assurance views

## Strategic Outcome

At 90 days, AtlasIT presents a unified automation platform with direct product adjacency across IT automation, compliance, trust center operations, third-party risk, and exposure management.
